What it is about

Akan names are derived from Ghananian culture where children are given their first names as a "day name" which matches to the day of the week that they were born.

Below is a list of days of the week with their Akan names and the gender to which they belong to:

How it works

The user is required to fill in his/her gender, the date, month and year of birth they were born in.


It will calculate these information and give out your Akan name
